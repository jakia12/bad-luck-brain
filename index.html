how to buy

create a wallet

Download phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.io.

get  some Sol

You will need Sol in your wallet to switch to Bad luck brain. If you don’t have any Sol, you can buy directly on phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Raydium

Connect to Raydium. Go to app.Raydium.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $BRIAN token address into Raydium, select $BRIAN, and confirm. When phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Swap

Switch Sol for $BRIAN. Welcome to the Bad luck brain family!
